我的诗译

《静夜思》

床前明月光，

疑是地上霜。

举头望明月，

低头思故乡。

- 李白

Moonlight before my bed,

Could it be frost on the ground?

I raise my head to view the bright moon,

I lower my head and think of home.

- Li Bai

《相思》

红豆生南国，

春来发几枝？

愿君多采撷，

此物最相思。

- 王维

Red beans grow in southern lands,

How many branches blossom in spring?

Gather them, my dear one,

As they evoke such deep longing.

- Wang Wei

《登鹳雀楼》

白日依山尽，

黄河入海流。

欲穷千里目，

更上一层楼。

- 王之涣

The white sun sets behind the mountains,

The Yellow River flows into the sea.

To enjoy a thousand-mile view,

Climb one more floor of the tower.

- Wang Zhihuan
